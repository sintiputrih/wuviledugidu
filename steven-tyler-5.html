Steven Victor Tallarico, popularly known as Steven Tyler, is an American singer, songwriter, actor, musician, and former television personality. As of 2023, Steven Tyler’s net worth is $150 million. He accumulated his net worth through professional music career by releasing music on streaming platforms and through albums and generated a handsome amount from live performances and collaborative music release.

Tyler is best known for serving as lead vocalist and playing Piano, Percussions, and Harmonica in the rock band Aerosmith, where his high screams and wide vocal range gave him a nickname called “Demon of Screamin”. Additionally, he found fame through performing acrobatics on stage, makeup, hairstyle and dresses in colourful, sometimes androgynous outfits.

His lead single “Dream On” provided him breakthrough as singer and musician as the single made 965 million streams on Spotify and generated 121 million views on YouTube music video. The band’s first number one hit single “I Don’t Want to Miss a Thing” also made over 900 million streams and is the only single to date which debuted on the top on Billboard Hot 100.

Tyler has been ranked among the list of 100 Greatest Singers of all time by Rolling Stone and his named placed on third spot on Hit Parader’s Top 100 Metal Vocalists of All Time. His contribution in the music industry honoured him with Rock and Roll Hall of Fame in 2001 and also inducted into the Songwriters Hall of Fame with song writing partner Joe Perry.
