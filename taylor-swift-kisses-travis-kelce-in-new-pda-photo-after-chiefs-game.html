This love is golden. Kansas City Chiefs gold, to be exact. Taylor Swift can be seen kissing Travis Kelce’s cheek in a precious PDA photo shared by Chariah Gordon, Chiefs wide receiver Mecole Hardman’s girlfriend. The “Lover” singer, 33, sweetly puts her hand on Travis’ chest and gives him a kiss on the cheek. Meanwhile, Travis, 34, has the biggest smile on his face.

In another photo, Taylor has her arm draped around Chariah as the group poses together after the October 22 game. Chariah and Taylor also pose for a WAGS-only photo. Taylor can be seen rocking a Kansas City Chiefs T-shirt, with her vintage Chiefs sweatshirt tied around her waist.

Taylor watched Travis and the Chiefs beat the Los Angeles Chargers 31 to 17 from a private suite. She cheered on the team with Brittany Mahomes, Patrick Mahomes’ wife. The new friends even showed off their special handshake after a key Chiefs play.

 

After the game, Taylor and Travis were spotted holding hands as they left Arrowhead Stadium. By that time, Travis had changed out of his uniform and into his red plaid shirt and khaki pants. The couple joined their friends at Travis’ Kansas City mansion for a post-game party.

The “Cruel Summer” singer’s romance with the tight end has been heating up over the last few weeks. Taylor has attended several of Travis’ games now, and they recently had a whirlwind weekend in New York City that included making cameos during the SNL premiere. The couple confirmed their romance when they showed off PDA during their date nights in the Big Apple.

Taylor and Travis have been spending as much time together as they can before their schedules get even busier. The Grammy winner kicks off the international leg of her Eras Tour on November 9 in Buenos Aires, Argentina. Since Travis has a bit of a break from football in the coming weeks, he’s planning on traveling to support his new love.

“Travis and Taylor are all in,” a source revealed to ET. “The two are very into each other and are enjoying their time together but are also planning for the future. Taylor starts her international tour in November, and Travis is planning to be there to spend time with her.”
