Kehlani Ashley Parrish, popularly known as Kehlani is an American who is a famous dancer, songwriter, and singer by profession. She rose to fame after becoming a member of the famous teen group named Poplyfe. She is in the industry since the year 2009.

In the year 2016, she received a Grammy nomination for her work in You Should Be Here for Best Urban Contemporary Album. She majorly works with the genres neo soul, hip hop, and R&B, and works with the labels HBK, /Atlantic, and Tsunami Mob.

Kehlani Wiki/Biography

Born on 24 April 1995, Kehlani’s age is 26 years as of September 2021. She was born and brought up in a lower-middle-class Christian family from Oakland, California, the United States. She is an American by nationality and has her belief in the Christianity religion.

She completed her early education at the Oakland School of Arts in Oakland, California, United States.

She later enrolled herself at the Julliard School in California, the United States to pursue her dream of being a professional dancer. From the very beginning of her childhood, she was more interested in co-curricular activities rather than studying.
