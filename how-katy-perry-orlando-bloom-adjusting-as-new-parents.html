Katy Perry and Orlando Bloom are relishing time spent with their new baby girl, Daisy Dove Bloom.

According to Entertainment Tonight, they "can't get enough" of their newborn daughter, whom they welcomed into the world last month.

"Since giving birth, the couple has been flooded with love and kindness. Their friends have been sending them gifts and words of encouragement non-stop. And due to COVID-19, the couple has FaceTimed with people and showed them Daisy," a source told the outlet.

Perry is also reportedly effortlessly adjusting to motherhood.

"[Katy] has been incredible with her daughter and is obsessed with her. The couple and their love for one another had grown to a level they didn't even expect since the arrival of their daughter," continued the source.

Perry and Bloom shared the news of their baby girl's arrival late last month with an Instagram post via the UNICEF Instagram account.

"We are floating with love and wonder from the safe and healthy arrival of our daughter," the couple said in their statement.
