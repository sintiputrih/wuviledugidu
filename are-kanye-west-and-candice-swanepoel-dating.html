Are Kanye West And Candice Swanepoel Dating?

According to Entertainment Tonight, Kanye West, who is co-parenting with ex-wife Kim, is now dating Candice Swanepoel. A source of the outlet revealed, “Kanye and Candice are dating and their relationship is new. They like each other and are happy with how things are going so far… Kanye is excited about Candice.”

While many are amused by this surprising romance, the insider further divulged, “They’ve connected over fashion and creativity.” Since they were seen together at New York Fashion Week, these two have been making headlines.

They were spotted hanging out and claimed they appeared to be flirting a lot. But could that be the most obvious indication of a romance, though?

As they both attended the Yeezy x Gap SHDZ party, Kanye and Candice reportedly left the party together and headed to the rapper’s hotel. These two are not only rumored to be dating but also collaborated in Kanye’s latest YZY SHDZ campaign, in which the model is featured.

On his Instagram, the rapper shared a picture of a bald-head Candice sporting brand-new silver YZY eyewear. Another picture from the same shoot that he uploaded showed her going topless.

However, there are sources who claimed that the two are not actually dating, but their relationship is more business than personal.

An insider of Us Weekly revealed, “They are together for promotional stuff for his Yeezy line. They collaborate well together and bond over fashion. Anything beyond a working relationship is not true. They are not involved romantically. She’s another one of his muses.”

While there are contradictory reports as to the dating rumors swirling around Kanye West and Candice Swanepoel, fans will have to wait for the two to tell us what their actual status is. In the meantime, it is best to take them with a grain of salt.

Is Kanye West Trying To Make Peace With The Kardashians?

Kanye West is having trouble moving past Kim Kardashian. The American rapper made Kris Jenner's photo his Instagram profile image, claiming it was done with “thoughts of peace and respect.”

The only clan member who continues to follow Kanye on the photo and video sharing app is Kris, even after their social media feud.

Many are shocked after Kanye changed his profile picture to a headshot image of Kris. Fortunately, he explained the reason behind it, saying that he did it with the best intentions, "I posted Kris Jenner with thoughts of peace and respect, Let’s change the narrative,” he said.

After what he did, fans flocked to social media to give their thoughts about it. One mocked him on Twitter, “Look love him or hate him he’s funny,” while another wrote, “When I think the obsession can’t possibly get worse.”

There were some who also thought that the rapper is doing that in an effort to mend ties with the family of his ex-wife Kim.

It can be remembered that Kris played a big role in her daughter Kim in handling her divorce from Kanye. The Kardashian mother said that the entire family stuck together for Kim during her marriage breakdown.

“When any one of us is going through something big in our lives, it’s really important for us to understand they’re not always going to have a lot of other people who are going to understand the situation,” Kris said.
