Born as Jacob Christopher Ortiz, Tito Ortiz is a former UFC Light Heavyweight Champion and retired mixed martial artist with an estimated net worth of $15 million as of 2012.

Born on January 23, 1975 in Santa Ana California, Tito Ortiz is of Mexican and American heritage. He attended the Golden West College where he continued with his wrestling career. Debuting in 1997, Tito Ortiz made a successful career in UFC. He has defeated Guy Mezger, Jeremy Screeton, and Jerry Bohlander.

Apart from being a UFC fighter, Tito Ortiz is also an actor. He has appeared on several film projects including Zombie Strippers, Cradle 2 the Grave,  The Crow: Wicked Prayer, and Valley of the Wolves: Iraq. He has also appeared on Hell’s Kitchen and Got the Life music video.

Tito is currently married to adult film star Jenna Jameson.
