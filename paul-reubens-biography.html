Who is Paul Reubens?

Paul Reubens is an American actor, writer, comedian, film producer, and game show host. People mostly recognize him for his famous portrayal of the character Pee-wee Herman.

Additionally, he has made cameos and guest appearances in numerous shows like ‘Reno 911!’, ‘Dirt’, and ‘The Blacklist’ among others.

Who are the parents of Paul Reubens?

Reubens was born in Peekskill, New York on August 27, 1952, to parents Judy (Rosen) and Milton Rubenfeld. Throughout most of his childhood years, he grew up in Sarasota, Florida, and Oneonta, New York.

His father was an automobile salesperson and his mother was a teacher. Additionally, he has two younger siblings, Luke and Abby.

He is of American nationality. Furthermore, he belongs to the Ashkenazi Jewish ethnic background.

Talking about his education, Reubens attended Sarasota High School. Later, he was enrolled at Northwestern University‘s summer program. Furthermore, he also attended Boston University.

In addition, he was also enrolled at the California Institute of the Arts.
