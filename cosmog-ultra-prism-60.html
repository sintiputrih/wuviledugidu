Cosmog (Japanese: コスモッグ Cosmog) is a Psychic-type Basic Pokémon card. It was first released as part of the Ultra Prism expansion.

Release information

This card was included in the Ultra Prism expansion, first released in the Japanese GX Battle Boost subset. It was reprinted as a Non Holofoil in the Japanese Psychic Mewtwo-GX Deck.
