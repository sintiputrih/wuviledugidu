Professional Life

At the age of 14, she moved to New York City, and worked at nightclubs like Danceteria and The Limelight, as a bartender.

While working the nightclubs, she met the fashion industry personnel and suggested trying her luck out in the fashion industry. She met famous photographer Steven Meisel and the table turned for her.

Furthermore, in 1997, she posed in Italian Vogue along with other popular models like Naomi Campbell, Amber Valetta, Kristen McMenamy, Carolyn Murphy, Vincent Gallo, and Kylie Bax.

She was a successful fashion model. Finally, she left her modeling career to do acting.
