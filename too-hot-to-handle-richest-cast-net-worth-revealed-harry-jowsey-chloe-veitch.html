The Richest 'Too Hot To Handle' Cast Members, Revealed

July 22, 2024
blog

In life, there are few things man is yet to completely crack and one of those is how love works. While several tests have been carried out to understand how it works, the emotion has repeatedly left us in awe, and we see this firsthand in reality shows today. In most cases, these shows are targeted at helping people but Netflix’s Too Hot to Handle gives us a new angle into how the feelings of love work.

Unlike Love Island and Are You The One, Too Hot to Handle follows a group of singles who are placed in a house for weeks to connect with each other but are forbidden from having any form sexual contact while they also go through several tasks to win the grand prize. Although this may not sound very difficult, the cast have proven just how much of a challenge it is. Speaking of its cast members, in addition to helping some of them find love, the show has also made them a lot richer than before. Here are the richest cast member from Too Hot To Handle.

10 Harry Jowsey's Net Worth Is $4.5 Million

Also known as "Wild-Child Harry," Jowsey has been at the top of the Los Angeles influencer scene for some time now. While on the show, he and fellow cast mate, Francesca Farago found love, but it didn’t last very long. Now, even amid reports of him being tied to other women romantically, Jowsey still gives his fans dating tips. Apart from being active on TikTok and Instagram with millions of followers, Jowsey also has a podcast where he puts out content with other Internet personalities. The endorsements that have come from his fame have earned him an estimated net worth of $4.5 million.

9 Francesca Farago's Net Worth Is $3 Million

Francesca Farago, the social media queen of all the Too Hot to Handle cast members, has had a really hard time finding love on the show, particularly with Harry Jowsey. Seeing as their relationship didn’t work out, Farago repositioned her energy into her brand and the results are pretty staggering. Apart from building her Instagram account to close to six million followers, she has also launched her beauty line called Farago The Label. While all of this is on paper, her paycheck also confirms her success as she has an estimated net worth of $3 million.

8 Cam Holmes' Net Worth Is $2 Million

Just like most of the previous participants of the show, Cam Holmes’ Instagram followership completely exploded after his appearance on the show. Apart from gaining more followers and all-round fame, Holmes also found love and is still happily in a relationship with Too Hot To Handle alumni, Emily Miller. Just like most of the other stars, Holmes has been raking in a lot of influencer money and is currently estimated to be worth $2 million.

7 David Birtwistle's Net Worth Is $1.8 Million

Before pursuing a degree in strength and conditioning at St Mary's University in London, David Birtwistle was a professional Rugby player at Wasps rugby club. He has also participated in different sports over the years and built a pretty impressive career. Now, he is a full-time fitness and nutrition coach, and if you think that doesn’t pay well, think again as Birtwistle is currently estimated to be worth $1.8 million.

6 Rhonda Paul's Net Worth Is $1.2 Million

Rhonda Paul had a considerably memorable time on Too Hot To Handle season one, and most fans will probably remember her for her spicy romance with Sharron Townsend and being one of the hottest single moms on the show. Before the show, she wasn’t very active on social media but now, she has around one million followers. While her followership helps her land advertising gigs now and then, she also runs a beauty service and jewelry selling business called PureLuXX. Paul’s net worth now has been estimated to be $1.2 million.

5 Emily Miller's Net Worth Is $1 Million

In season two of Too Hot To Handle, Emily Miller’s relationship with Cam Holmes rose to become one of the most interesting parts of the show. Till date, the pair are still perceived as one of the luckiest cast members because not only did they find love on the show, but they also gained major fame. On her own, Miller has been consistently dazzling fans with her beauty off the show and as expected, it helped launch her into the world of influencing. This helped her make a lot of extra cash and that has now brought her net worth up to $1 million.

4 Kelechi Dyke's Net Worth Is $1 Million

Mostly known by the name Kelz, he rose to fame after his appearance in search of love in the first installment of Too Hot To Handle. Aside from being a reality TV star, he is also a senior recruitment consultant and a professional athlete. While he earns good money from his day job, Kelz is also a model and has worked with several top brands. His net worth is presently estimated to Stan at $1 million.

3 Madison Wyborny's Net Worth Is $1 Million

Some believe Madison Wyborny getting kicked off the show along with Kori Sampson was because she joined much later in episode 6 of the first installment, while others believe she didn’t put in as much work. Regardless, Wyborny did get her share of fame and that has probably put more money in her pocket than the grand prize, as she currently has a net worth of $1 million.

2 Bryce Hirschberg's Net Worth Is $850,000

Unlike most of the other stars who got their fame after the show, Bryce Hirschberg was already doing well in the entertainment industry professionally before the show. Hirschberg is credited as an actor, film director, and producer with several international awards including Best Feature Narrative at the Wolves Independent International Film Awards. His career in the industry has been going smoothly and has given him a net worth of $850,000.

1 Chloe Veitch's Net Worth Is $400,000

Chloe Veitch wasn’t exactly lucky enough to find love on the show, but she did manage to prove to us that she had exactly what it takes to be a reality TV queen. With around two million Instagram followers, Veitch has climbed her way to the top. With an influencing career that features makeup routines, paid promotions, and travel vlogs, Veitch now has an estimated net worth of $400,000. Additionally, the star landed a spot on Netflix's social media competition show, The Circle, and she has also been announced as the host of the third season of Too Hot To Handle.
