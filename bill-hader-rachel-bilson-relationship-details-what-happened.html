The OC actress Rachel Bilson and Barry creator Bill Hader were getting serious about their romance when the coronavirus pandemic put a strain on their relationship. The acting duo were first linked in fall 2019 and made their red carpet debut at the Golden Globes in January 2020.

8 How Did Rachel Bilson And Bill Hader Meet?

The film was written and directed by Bill Hader's then-wife Maggie Carey. The couple split in 2017, after 11 years of marriage and share three children, Hannah, 10, Harper, 7, and Hayley, 5. Their divorce was finalized in March 2018.
