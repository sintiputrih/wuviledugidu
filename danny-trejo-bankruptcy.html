How did Danny Trejo go bankrupt?

Danny Trejo submitted a Chapter 11 personal bankruptcy petition in February 2023. Danny filed the paperwork after accruing an estimated $2 million in IRS debt.

In an interview he provided to TMZ on the day of the filing, he claimed that the debt was primarily due to erroneous deductions.
