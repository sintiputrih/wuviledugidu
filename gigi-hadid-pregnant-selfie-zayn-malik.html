Pregnant Gigi Hadid Shares Photo of Shirtless Zayn Malik for Charity

July 30, 2024
blog
  • Gigi Hadid shared a new selfie taken with boyfriend, Zayn Malik.
  • Hadid is taking part in charity auction, The Way We Wore, to raise money for the NAACP and NHS Charities Together.
  • Hadid is pregnant with her first child.

Gigi Hadid is joining forces with British Vogue to auction off one of her outfits for charity. The Way We Wore auction will raise funds for the NAACP and National Health Service Charities Together.

Other models taking part in the auction include Helena Christensen, Joan Smalls, Lily Aldridge, and Christy Turlington. However, it was Hadid's selfie that caught our attention because she could be seen posing with a shirtless Zayn Malik.

In the cute photo, the pregnant model snapped a mirror selfie as she embraced her beau. Malik and Hadid are currently expecting their first baby together.

Of Hadid's pregnancy, a source told Entertainment Tonight, "Gigi has kept the secret close to her family and friends for a while, as she’s only a few months along. Once Gigi and Zayn got back together at the end of last year, it was like they never skipped a beat and knew what they had was special. The couple and their families are overjoyed."

During an interview on The Tonight Show with Jimmy Fallon. Hadid discussed her pregnancy and said, "Obviously, you know, we wish we could’ve announced it on our own terms, but we’re very excited and happy and grateful for everyone’s well wishes and support, so thank you."
