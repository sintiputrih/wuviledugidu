Who is John Fox?

John Fox is an American football coach and former player.

He has worked as a head coach for the Carolina Panthers, Denver Broncos and Chicago Bears of the National Football League.

Who are the parents of John Fox?

Fox was born in Virginia Beach, Virginia on February 8, 1955, to parents Ron Fox and Kaye Fox. He became interested in football since his childhood years.

He is of American nationality. Additionally, there are no details about his ethnic background at present.

Talking about his education, Fox attended Castle Park High School and Southwestern College.

Furthermore, he also attended the San Diego State University. He has a bachelor’s degree in physical education.

John Fox’s Career, Salary and Net Worth

Fox initially played football at Castle Park High School. later, he signed a two-year contract with the Tampa Bay Buccaneers. Eventually, he retired from playing football. He became the defensive backs coach at U.S. International University. Additionally, he was also the defensive backs coach for the Boise State University Broncos in 1980. After entering NFL in 1989, he was the secondary coach of the Pittsburgh Steelers.

Fox became the third head coach of the Carolina Panthers in 2002 and he got selected as the 14th head coach of the Denver Broncos on January 13, 2011. Furthermore, he became the head coach of Chicago Bears on January 16, 2015.
