Liv Morgan Acknowledges Struggles Faced in 2024 WWE Royal Rumble Return

February 24, 2024
blog

Liv Morgan was away from WWE television for a long time due to injury but that changed after she returned at the Royal Rumble. It seems she openly admitted that making a comeback to the Rumble was a personal struggle for her.

Liv Morgan made her triumphant return to WWE last night during the Women’s Royal Rumble match, much to the delight of fans all over the world. She was among the final four women in the match but was unable to win the whole thing in the end.

While speaking with Denise Salcedo in an exclusive interview, Liv Morgan talked about her return at the Royal Rumble Premium Live Event. Morgan admitted she had to face a lot of struggles and it was a fight for her to return at the Rumble.

“It’s been a journey for the last six months. It’s been a fight, it’s been a struggle. So to come back at the Royal Rumble at the coveted number 30 spot, I was very happy and I felt honored. Just happy to be back, happy to be back doing what I love, doing my thing. Came really, really, really close but I’m happy for Bayley nonetheless.”

Liv Morgan also broke longstanding record at the Royal Rumble Premium Live Event. We will have to wait and see what’s next in store for Liv Morgan, now that she is back for good since fans just want her to be booked properly this time around.
