Jon Actor

Jon’s first film role came at the age of 11 when he portrayed the Boy Horace in the Charlton Heston film Will Penny, which was written and directed by his father. He played the role of Lazlo Hollyfield in Real Genius (1985), Shawn McDermott in the TV series Martin (1992-1994), Harvey in The Rundown, and Ronnie Wingate in Get Shorty (1995). He also lent his voice for the video game Hitman: Absolution.

Jon had a guest role on The X-Files in the episode “Sleepless”. He portrayed Dylan McKay’s drug dealer in season 5 of Beverly Hills, 90210. He portrayed Ben Linus’ father Roger in an ongoing role on the TV series Lost. He was cast in the CSI: NY episode “The Box” as the father of that episode’s victim in 2008. He wrapped production on A True Story in 2010

Jon also portrayed Martin in the TV Show Supernatural in the episodes including Sam, Interrupted (2010), Hello, Cruel World (2011), and Citizen Fang (2012) and played the role of Bob, a former drug addict who operates a safe haven for abused Mexican women, in The Bridge.
