Lindsay Buckingham Biography

Lindsay Buckingham is an American journalist who is currently a Special Projects Reporter at WKYC studios in Cleveland. She specializes in uplifting, inspiring, and hopeful stories. A Rocky River, Ohio native, she attended Rocky River High School. Later on, she attended and graduated from Columbia College Chicago with a degree in journalism.

After graduating, she interned at WBBM-TV, working closely under award-winning investigative journalists, Dave Savini and Pam Zekman for more than a year. Later ib she joined FOX8 in Cleveland in 2007. While here, she covered the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame induction ceremony, NBA Finals games, MLB playoffs games, the Republican National Convention, and various community and city events.

After several years of service at FOX8, she left for a position at 3News. Buckingham joined 3News in May 2018. She is currently the station’s Special Projects Reporter specializing in uplifting, inspiring, and hopeful stories.
