Entrepreneurial Experience

After Ramaswamy graduated from Yale, his professional life took off. He experimented with biotechnology by helping to start Roivant Sciences. He had proven his commercial savvy by becoming a multimillionaire biotech executive by the year 2020.

Republican presidential candidate

The mission of Ramaswamy started in February 2023. His successful presidential campaign helped him become a prominent figure in the race.

Recognizability and Popularity
