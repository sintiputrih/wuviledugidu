Justin Travis McBride (born August 7, 1979) is a former professional bull rider who competed on the PBR’s Built Ford Tough Series Tour. He is now a member of the PBR’s Built Ford Tough Series Tour. He is a two-time PBR World Champion (2005 and 2007), with a career-high 32 victories, and was the first professional bull rider to earn more than $5 million in earnings over the course of his professional bull riding career. The country music singer and colour commentator for the Built Ford Tough Series telecasts on CBS Sports Network is well known for his work as a country music artist.
