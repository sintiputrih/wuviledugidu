Randy Orton

Randy became the second-youngest two-time WWE Champion when he won two WWE Championships in one night. Moreover, this wrestler was only 27 years old at the time. In 2008, he established the group The Legacy alongside Ted DiBiase Jr. and Cody Rhodes.
