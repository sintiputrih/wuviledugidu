Lena Waithe Wiki/Biography

Born on 17th May 1984, her age is 37 years as of 2021. She was born and brought up in Chicago, Illinois, United States.

She completed her elementary education from Magnet School which was a predominantly African-American School and later went to Chute Middle School in Evanston and Evanston Township High School, Illinois.

She completed her higher education from Columbia College in Chicago. She earned a graduation degree in Cinema and Television arts from there. She was working in major retail outlets like Best Buy and Blockbuster before she decides to move to Los Angeles in search of a better future.
