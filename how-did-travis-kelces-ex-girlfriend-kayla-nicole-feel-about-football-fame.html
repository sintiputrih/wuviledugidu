What Did Travis Kelce's Ex-Girlfriend Kayla Nicole Say About His Fame?

Although it can be tough and tricky to date a well-known football player, Kayla Nicole doesn't seem to have commented on her ex-boyfriend Travis Kelce's fame.

Kelce's ex Maya Benberry called him a "narcissist," but Kayla Nicole hasn't talked very much about her relationship or her breakup. Of course, that doesn't mean that she always loved the experience of being in a relationship with him, or that she liked how much people talked about her as a result. But unless she wants to share with her fanbase what it was really like to date Travis Kelce, people will be left in the dark.

According to Sportskeeda, Nicole might have spoken about Kelce on her Instagram account at least once. She posted a photo of herself and wrote in the caption, "Talking bout 'I want to see other people.' Yea ok boy, pick your fighter." It's possible that this is what Kelce said to her when they broke up, but since she didn't explain more, people can only speculate.

When Kayla Nicole shared a video on Instagram and talked about going through hard times, she might have been thinking about her breakup. However, she didn't mention Travis Kelce specifically.

According to The Daily Mail, she said, "Kayla here, It's always been really important to me to use my platform not to create division but to elevate and unite women - black women specifically. So I prepared a letter and would like to share it with you today."

Nicole continued, "I'm reminded that this isn't just about me. This isn't just my story. A singular experience. My truth, trauma and vulnerability is a relatable part of the human experience. I know I'm not alone. And I want to make sure you know you're not either. On days where I feel most inadequate, I have to remind myself that I am, have been and will always be more than enough."

Nicole concluded with, "dear black girl, when the world gets dark and times get hard and when you feel like your back is against the wall and your hands are tied like nobody knows, understands or knows what it takes to be you. Remember that you are a part of something way bigger."

It seems likely that if Kayla Nicole found it difficult to date Travis Kelce because he is so famous, she would have said so. Although it's hard to find any interviews with her online since she mostly posts on her social media accounts, she does speak up when she has something important to say. She often shares these thoughts on her Twitter account.

For instance, The Sun reported in January 2023 that people said that Kelce was "cheap." He explained when he was interviewed on The Pivot Podcast, "I would never say that I was supporting her. She didn't like that he said that and tweeted that women should never be obligated to make a meal for someone who they are dating. She then tweeted, "I don’t have 'beef' with anyone. I have beef with the ideation that reduces a woman’s worth to what’s being put on a dinner plate. This narrative was publicly shared on a podcast, it’s important for it 2 be publicly denounced. Now if y’all will excuse me, gonna go order postmates."
