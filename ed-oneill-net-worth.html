Ed O’Neill | Career

Ed was born in Youngstown, Ohio, to a homemaker and truck driver on April 12, 1946.

He went to Ursuline High School but transferred to Worthington High School.

After high school, he attended Ohio University on a football scholarship.

However, after his sophomore year, he transferred to Youngstown State University.

Ed was a defensive lineman on the football team.

After graduation, he worked as a substitute teacher at his alma mater Ursuline High School.

In 1977, the actor moved to New York to embark acting career.
