Who is Dylan Sprouse?

Dylan Sprouse is an American actor. He was born on August 4, 1992, in Arezzo, Italy. He was born 15 minutes before his twin brother, Cole Sprouse. His full name is Dylan Thomas Sprouse.

Quick facts

Educational background

Dylan Sprouse attended New York University’s Gallatin School of Individualized Study along with his younger brother, Cole. While Cole graduated with a degree in Archeology, Dylan graduated with a four-year degree in video game design. 

Relationships and dating history

Dylan Sprouse has been dating model Barbara Palvin since the summer of 2018. Before this, Dylan was in a relationship with model Dayna Frazer. Palvin and Sprouse started talking to each other on Instagram after the actor sent Palvin a direct message.

They felt a genuine connection between them and soon began a relationship. By January 25, 2019, the love birds had moved in together as they had bought their first apartment together in New York. The couple celebrated their three-year anniversary on June 16, 2021. 

Dylan Sprouse career

Acting career

Dylan has been involved in entertainment since he was eight months old. His maternal grandmother, Jonine Wright, suggested his parents, Matthew Sprouse and Melanie Wright, involve Dylan and his twin brother in showbiz since Jonine herself was a drama teacher and actress. Cole and Dylan worked together in many commercials, TV shows, and movies where the two of them would often play a single role. 

Dylan and his brother gained media attention after they played the role of Julian in the comedy film Big Daddy where they appeared alongside Adam Sandler. They later starred together in the hit Disney Channel sitcom titled The Suite Life of Zack and Cody. The brothers also reprised their roles of Zack Martin and Cody Martin in the show’s  2008 spinoff, The Suite Life on Deck. 

After graduating from college, both Dylan and Cole started focusing on their solo careers in order to break away from their existing image as the Sprouse brothers. In 2014, Dylan became a commentator on a few of the ‘Super Smash Bros’ tournaments. 

Apart from this, Dylan was cast as Lucas Ward in the 2017 thriller film Dismissed. He was also cast in the film Banana Split. In addition, he has also appeared in the movie After We Collided and the short film Daddy.  In 2021, it was announced that Dylan would be playing the role of Nico in The Sex Lives of College Girls, an upcoming teen comedy-drama TV series created by Mindy Kaling.
