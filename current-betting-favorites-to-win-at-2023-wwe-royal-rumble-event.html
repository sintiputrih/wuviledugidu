Current Betting Favorites To Win At 2023 WWE Royal Rumble Event

August 26, 2024

The 2023 Royal Rumble event is set to take place tonight from the Alamodome in San Antonio, Texas, Just like every year, the focal point of this year’s event is the men’s and women’s Royal Rumble matches. Apart from this, there are some fantastic matches planned for the night, including the first-ever pitch-black match and the Undisputed WWE Universal Championship match between Kevin Owens and Roman Reigns. The betting odds of the respective matches have been announced.

According to BetOnline, Cody Rhodes and Rhea Ripley are the odds on favorite to win their respective Royal Rumble matches. There are also no title changes expected, as Roman Reigns is the favorite to defeat Kevin Owens.

For the RAW Women’s Championship, Bianca Belair is expected to come out on top against Alexa Bliss, while Bray Wyatt is expected to defeat LA Knight in the pitch-black match.
