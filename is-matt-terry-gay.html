Is Matt Terry Gay?

No, Matt Terry is not gay. The rumors surrounding Matt Terry’s sexuality sent shockwaves through the internet, especially after an incident where he was alleged to have kissed his friend Freddy shortly after his X Factor victory.

However, Matt wasted no time in setting the record straight. In an interview with Metro, he candidly confirmed, “I’m heterosexual.”

He went on to express his genuine affection for Freddy, emphasizing that their connection was purely spiritual and not romantic: “I love Freddy to pieces. We hit it off from the beginning. We’re just really close. We didn’t kiss on stage.”

Matt Terry has maintained a refreshingly open-minded perspective when it comes to these speculations about his sexuality. In his words:

“It doesn’t bother me – who cares? If you like a boy, you like a boy, if you like a girl, you like a girl, if you like both, you like both… it’s 2016.”

He also emphasized that there are far more important things to worry about than someone’s sexual orientation, and he takes no offense in being labeled otherwise.

Despite the rumors, Matt’s living arrangement with Freddy raised more questions. Freddy eventually moved back to his hometown in 2017, but that hasn’t stopped fans from speculating about Matt’s sexuality.

Nevertheless, Matt has consistently maintained that he is heterosexual. In an interview with OK! magazine, he even chuckled at the persistent gay rumors.

His track record of dating women in the past stands as a testament to his straight sexual orientation, putting the speculation to rest.
