25 Black Actresses Over 50 You Should Follow in 2023

March 31, 2024
blog

Black actresses over 50 have made some of the most amazing impacts on Black Cinema and Hollywood as a whole. Over the years, these actresses have also aged like fine wine, often looking as young as when they started out but have amassed accomplishments and awards that make them stand out. Take a look at some of the most fabulous black actresses over 50 that you should follow.

Famous Black Actresses Over 50

1. Gabrielle Union

  • Date of Birth: October 29, 1972
  • Age: 51 Years Old
  • Spouses: Chris Howard (2001–2006), Dwyane Wade (m. 2014)
  • Children: One (Kaavia James)

Gabrielle Union made her acting debut in on Broadway in 1996, even though she had been acting in school productions. Her breakthrough role came in the teen comedy 10 Things I Hate About You (1999), where she played the character Chastity. This early success paved the way for her to establish herself as a prominent actress in Hollywood.

Gabrielle Union is married to former professional basketball player Dwyane Wade but was previously married to retired professional American football running back Chris Howard. She shares a daughter with Wade, who she had through a surrogate. She is also a stepmother to his three children from his other relationships.

  • Age: 52 Years Old

Jada Pinkett Smith has taken on diverse and impactful roles in films like The Nutty Professor, Ali, and The Matrix film franchise. Pinkett Smith has been married to Will Smith since 1997. They have two children, Jaden Smith (born 1998) and Willow Smith (born 2000).

She has also found success on the small screen, earning a Primetime Emmy Award for her portrayal of blues singer Bessie Smith in the HBO biographical film Bessie.

She is also a successful businesswoman, having founded a production company, Flavor Unit Entertainment, which has produced several films and television shows. She also owns a clothing line, Flavor Unit Jeans, and her own fragrance line, Queen Latifah.
