Netflix’s Single’s Inferno, which debuted its successful first season on December 18, 2021, is returning for season 2 with a new cast of handsome single castaways, one of whom is the stunning Lee So-e.

If you’re not familiar with the plot of the program, stunning singles search for love on a remote island named “Inferno.” To escape the inferno and go to “paradise,” a place with sumptuous mansions where they may enjoy their new connection, the competitors must establish a romantic bond with another person and persuade them to reciprocate. They can disclose more intimate details like their ages and employment while enjoying a romantic date there. The work is challenging, though, and romance is difficult to come by because so much drama occurs.

Basically, the show is the Korean spin-off of Too Hot To Handle. The show became the first-ever Korean reality series to make Netflix’s global top 10.

Lee So-e On Netflix’s Single’s Inferno Season 2

Lee So-e has been introduced as an adorable and silent one among all the newly introduced 9 cast members. While other girls are often attractive or possess strong personalities, she is comfortable being who she is in real life. She has expressed that she can be clumsy at times, but she is a genuine and kind sweetheart.

Coming to what she came in looking for in a guy while appearing on the show, So-e likes tall guys with strong features. “There’s something about being in their arms and feeling safe,” she says. And, they have to be generous and reliable.

So-e adds that her attractive feature is her ability to laugh a lot and being a good listener. During her personal interview, Lee revealed an embarrassing secret: she believes she is rather “popular.” Before joining the show, she has dated only once who was also her first crush.

The other cast members joining her this season are Shin Seul-ki, Choi Jong-woo, Park Se-jeong, Jo Yoong-jae, Lee Nadine, Kim Han-bin, Shin Dong-woo, and Choi Seo-eun. Season 2 of ‘Single’s Inferno’ stars Kyuhyun, Lee Da Hee, Hong Jin Kyung, and Hanhae as MCs.

Is Lee So-e Dating Anyone?

At the end of the first episode, she was the only one who didn’t receive a postcard or in other words, none of the boys were interested. It was heartbreaking to see, especially since it is obvious that she has feelings for Jo Yoong-jae, the group’s leading man. However, Yoong-jae picked Park Se-jeong.

Sadly, she couldn’t make it to the list of the first couple going to Paradise based on the first two episodes.
