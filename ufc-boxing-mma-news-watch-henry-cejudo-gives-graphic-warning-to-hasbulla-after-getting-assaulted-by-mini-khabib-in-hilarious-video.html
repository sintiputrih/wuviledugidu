Hasbulla Magomedov became an internet sensation through his TikTok videos. The star is often seen with UFC’s big personalities. He is a member of Khabib Nurmagomedov’s team. His association with the ‘Eagle’ is well known in the UFC world. Recently, he playfully scuffled with Henry Cejudo.

Henry Cejudo, popularly known as ‘Triple C’ or ‘The Messenger’, has previously interacted with ‘Mini Khabib’ a couple of times. However, his recent scuffle with Nurmagomedov’s teammate baffled the combat sports universe. The former UFC fighter and freestyle wrestler playfully sparred with ‘Mini Khabib’, while the pint-sized sensation kept on thrashing him.

Henry Cejudo and Hasbulla Magomedov playfully fight

Henry Cejudo recently uploaded a video clip showing his interaction with Hasbulla Magomedov on Instagram. The video began with Cejudo messing around with ‘Mini Khabib’, while Magomedov seemingly thrashed the former mixed martial artist. 

‘Triple C’ wrote in the caption, “WARNING: GRAPHIC CONTENT. Here’s footage of @hasbulla.hushetskiy blatantly assaulting me. I won’t press charges as long as he SIGNS THE DAMN CONTRACT. It’s time to settle this once and for all. I’M COMING FOR YOU HASBULLA!!”

However, the social media sensation, Magomedov, didn’t back down from playfully commenting on the post. He wrote, “why didn’t you expose where I smeared you, you expose where you hit me.” The video ended with Cejudo sitting on a chair with Magomedov on his lap, giving punch after punch. 

‘Mini Khabib’ in the UFC

The 19-year-old viral sensation has signed a deal with the UFC. Previously, Magomedov took to Instagram and confirmed that he signed a 5-year contract with Ultimate Fighting Championship. He wrote, “The rumors are true. I have officially signed a deal to fight in the UFC! Details will be revealed by the end of the year.” Furthermore, he took this chance to advertise the ‘Hasbulla Fight Kit’. Additionally, he thanked Dana White, Khabib Nurmagomedov, and everyone who helped him get the contract.

The UFC president revealed the fact in an interview with Robbie Fox. White said, “I didn’t know this was coming, but yes we did sign Hasbulla. And what he’s going to end up [whether he fights or not] doing remains to be seen.” 

Magomedov’s rivalry with Abdu Rozik makes him one of the obvious opponents. Nonetheless, UFC fans are yet to know what Magomedov has to reveal. Fans indeed showed excitement at the news of Magomedov stepping into the Octagon as a fighter.
